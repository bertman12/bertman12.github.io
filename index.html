Grilled Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 1 adults
Ingredients Quantity
Wheat Bread
Peanut Butter
Jelly
Butter
Cheese 		3 slices
3 tbsp
3 tbsp
2 tbsp
4 slices

Step 1:Preheat frying pan on stove to medium temperature.

Step 2:Lather 2 slices of bread on one side with peanut butter and jelly. Lather both sides of the 3rd slice of bread to use as the middle slice. Distribute evenly.

Step 3: Assemble the slices of bread together while placing 2 slices of cheese underneath and on top of the middle slice of bread.

Step 4: Cook butter in the frying pan, spreading it where the sandwich will go.

Step 5: Place the sandwich in the frying pan. Continue to flip sandwich until both slices of bread achieve a golden brown color.

Step 6: Remove from pan, cut into 2 halves, and enjoy!
